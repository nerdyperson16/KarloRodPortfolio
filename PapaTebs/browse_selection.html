Here at PapaTebs Mask Company, we're striving to ensure that your masks are made in a fast, simple manner. That's why we're changing our delivery preferences to get your masks to your destination as soon as possible.

That's why we're changing how we are delivering our masks to you with the United States Postal Service.

PapaTebs Mask Company is now shifting towards the "First Class Package Service" delivery method instead of "Priority Mail" for now on.

This doesn't mean we're entirely shifting to "First Class Package Service". If you contact your PapaTebs Mask Company agent, you can opt-in for express shipping as an extra cost for your masks to get it there as soon as possible. Now, there are 2 methods of shipping... Express Shipping & Priority Mail Shipping.

Now, there are pros and cons towards these different methods. First Class Package Service might take a bit longer (depending on zones, these can differ). But, we now have the ability to cut down on packaging and provide you with decorations that can put a smile on your face. Priority mail offers a faster way to ship masks also which can take up to 2 days also.

Of course, we might ship your masks with a priority mail box which is offered to you at no extra charge depending on the case.

So, will this affect you? Probably not. If you need your masks immediately, you can definitely opt-in for our other shipping methods. But, we will always ship your masks thru First Class Package Service. Depending in your area, this can take upto 3-4 business days or even a week. You can request a tracking number by contacting an agent regarding this.

Thanks for choosing PapaTebs Mask Company. Stay safe out there.
